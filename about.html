The Photographer

Surya Narayan Behera

Based in the heart of Bhadrak, Odisha, Surya Narayan Behera is more than a photographer — he is a visual storyteller who translates human emotion into timeless digital artifacts.

With over a decade of dedicated practice, Surya has developed a signature style characterized by atmospheric depth, authentic candid moments, and an uncanny ability to capture the quiet moments that define a legacy.

His work at Gurudev Photography reflects his core philosophy: every session is a unique, gallery-worthy masterpiece waiting to be curated. From grand wedding celebrations to intimate portraits, Surya approaches every frame with reverence.
